The Daily Drip Coffee Shop Meeting

Brainstorming Spring Menu

04.12.2023

Introduction

Welcome to The Daily Drip 's weekly faculty meeting mintues!
The Daily Drip is a famous coffee shop local to Carlsbad, California. This website is dedicated to a particular meeting our faculty recently had about brainstorming our Spring menu. On this page you can find the meeting's attendence list, agenda, unfinished business, new business, questions/concerns, and 30-second video recording .

Meeting Style Details This is a super fun meeting style our cafe faculty does every new season!

Meeting Agenda

Here is the important list of items to be discussed this meeting:

  1. 5-minute refreshments. Coffee shop foods and beverages provided.
  2. Check-in with each faculty member.
  3. Review the coffee shop experiences, needs, and culture we want for the Spring Season.
  4. Brainstorm new coffee drinks and food for the Spring Season.
  5. Assess famous+popular menu items that should continue.
  6. Finalize a rough draft of the Spring Menu.

Notes

Unfinished Business

The Daily Drip's faculty meeting had unfinished items that are priority to complete next meeting. In no particular order, each is listed here:

  • Full cost of the Spring Menu.
  • A finalized version of the Spring Menu.
  • Information about the ingredients needed for new items.
  • Information about continuing relationships with producers for continuing products in the Spring Menu.

New Business

The Daily Drip's faculty meeting had a lot of finished items from the meeting. In no particular order, each is listed here:

  • Reviewed the asthetic for the coffee shop's Spring Menu.
  • Created new coffee drinks and food ideas for the menu.
  • Assessed popular items on the current menu.
  • Finished a rough draft of the Spring Menu.

Questions & Concerns

Here are the concerns and questions that came up from the meeting:

  • How much money can our coffee shop allocate to all new beverages & food options?
  • How easy will it be to source the ingredients for each item?
  • Will new food items or beverages be enjoyed by a majority of customers?
  • Will it be difficult for the baristas and chefs to make any specific items?
